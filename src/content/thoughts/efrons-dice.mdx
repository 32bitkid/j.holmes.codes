I've been trying to come up with a good mechanic for integrating [Non-transitive dice](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Intransitive_dice) into a TTRPG as a core mechanic. Non-transitive dice have always fascinated me. There are some very interesting forms of intransitive dice that I think could be really useful and subversive, especially given how much there is an expectation of "fair" dice rolls in a lot of TTRPG games.
