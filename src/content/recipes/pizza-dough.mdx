---
name: "Pizza Dough"
servings: "one serving"
---

* <data>750 g</data> bread flour
* <data>500 ml</data> water
* <data>25 g</data> olive oil
* <data>13 g</data> salt
* <data>5 g</data> honey
* <data>3 g</data> yeast

1. <small>Heat</small> water to ~110&deg; F
1. <small>Mix</small> warm water, yeast, and honey
1. <small>Bloom</small> yeast mixture for 10 to 15 minutes

{/* */}

1. <small>Combine</small> flour and salt in large bowl
1. <small>Pour</small> yeast mixture into flour
1. <small>Combine</small> flour and water with hands, or dough scraper, until combined
1. <small>Drizzle</small> olive oil and knead
1. <small>Rest</small> dough for 30 minutes

{/* */}

1. <small>Remove</small> rested dough
1. <small>Knead</small> dough by hand for 15 minutes
1. <small>Form</small> into large ball
1. <small>Coat</small>, lightly, with olive oil
1. <small>Cover</small> dough with plastic wrap
1. <small>Rise</small> for 1 hour

{/* */}

1. <small>Remove</small> plastic wrap
1. <small>Form</small> into six ~220g portions
1. <small>Rise</small> dough balls for 3 to 4 hours at room temperature, or 8 to 24 hours in the refrigerator

{/* */}

1. <small>Preheat</small> oven to 500&deg; F
1. <small>Place</small> single dough ball onto floured work surface
1. <small>Stretch</small>, top, and bake 7 minutes, or until desired doneness
