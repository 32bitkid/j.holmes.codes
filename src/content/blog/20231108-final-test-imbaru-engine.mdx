## The Process

After picking up "The Truth" and the "Lie" buffs, you can interact with the
four hive statues around the room. Your *goal* is to find out which runes are
**triangles**, and which are **circles**. There are a total of 16 runes. In _each_ puzzle room,
there are four statues. Once a statue has been activated, *three* runes will highlight, showing
either a **circle** or a **triangle** symbol. However, each _set-of-three_ hints includes
two-lies and one-truth. Meaning, two of those "hints" are **wrong** and one is **correct**.

## Solution

Once you have all the symbols for each room, you can correlate them together to
determine which hints were the _truth_, and which were _lies_. All symbols
are accounted for in the final solution and you need to _activate_ only the
**triangle** runes—matching the triangle symbol on the podium—to pass the final test.
